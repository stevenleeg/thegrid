welcome

the grid is a multiplayer real-time strategy game. your goal is to exterminate any other colonies on the grid by placing special tiles and collecting resources.

let me play!

the game is currently in its early stages of development. because of this, we're only looking a small number of people to play and test the game for feedback.

if you're interested in the game, feel free to be a tester.

anyone with some development experience can also get a local copy of the game running by cloning our github repository
