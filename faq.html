faq

When will the full game be released?

This is undecided at the moment. Right now we're focusing on development more than timelines. For the latest information, follow us on twitter.

How will I play the game?

The game is completely browser based. To play, just point your browser at the URL!

What browsers are supported?

Many cutting-edge web technologies are used throughout the game, therefor thegrid will most likely not run on anything but a recently updated browser. We only test on and officially support Google Chrome and Apple Safari.
